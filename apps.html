Apps in the Paperlight Suite

The Paperlight Suite is a growing collection of small, focused tools created under the QuietLoom name. All apps are designed to be offline-first, file-based, and calm.

BookLoom

Your quiet companion for every story you read. BookLoom is a simple, offline reading tracker designed for book lovers who value focus and privacy. Track your books, monitor your progress, sort your favorites, and reflect on what you’ve read — all without an account, cloud syncing, or distractions. Just you and your reading life, woven together beautifully.

Status: Available · Version 1.0

View details

StoryLoom (planned)

A cozy creative space for stories, worlds, and ideas. Whether you’re building a fantasy world or jotting down notes for your next chapter, StoryLoom keeps everything organized and offline. Craft characters, plots, timelines, and ideas in a space that feels like a writer’s notebook — quiet, intentional, and completely yours.

Status: Early concept

PaperLoom (planned)

A simple, paper-like notes app for thoughts, lists, and ideas. PaperLoom captures your notes quickly and quietly, just like a favorite notebook. Organize thoughts, brainstorm ideas, draft writing, or keep lists — all locally, with no accounts or syncing. Designed to stay out of your way and let your words breathe.

BloomLoom (planned)

Gentle habit and mood tracking for slow, steady growth. BloomLoom supports your routines and emotional wellbeing with a calm, minimalist design. Track daily habits, log your moods, and reflect on your progress — all without notifications, gamification, or judgment. A soft place to grow at your own pace.

LifeLoom (planned)

A calm daily organizer for tasks, routines, and schedules. LifeLoom helps you plan your days without chaos or digital noise. Create task lists, organize routines, outline schedules, and keep your life flowing at a comfortable pace. A peaceful alternative to cluttered productivity apps.

NestLoom (planned)

A soft, spacious home for your money — offline, simple, and stress-free. NestLoom helps you manage your budget without pressure, clutter, or the cloud. Track expenses, plan ahead, and build financial clarity in an environment that feels safe and calm. Your data stays with you — always.
