What is Paperlight?

Paperlight is a philosophy as much as a suite of tools. The goal is to make software that feels like a well-loved notebook: simple, dependable, and quiet.

Every app in the Paperlight Suite runs locally, stores data in human-readable files, and respects your attention. No notifications, no timelines, no feeds.
