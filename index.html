How it looks

What is Paperlight?

Paperlight is a philosophy as much as a suite of tools. The goal is to make software that feels like a well-loved notebook: simple, dependable, and quiet.

Every app in the Paperlight Suite runs locally, stores data in human-readable files, and respects your attention. No notifications, no timelines, no feeds.

BookLoom

Your quiet companion for every story you read. BookLoom is a simple, offline reading tracker designed for book lovers who value focus and privacy. Track your books, monitor your progress, sort your favorites, and reflect on what you’ve read — all without an account, cloud syncing, or distractions. Just you and your reading life, woven together beautifully.
