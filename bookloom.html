BookLoom 1.0

BookLoom is a cozy, terminal-based reading tracker. It helps you keep track of the books you want to read, what you’re currently reading, what you’ve finished, and which ones you chose not to finish.

Everything is stored locally in simple JSON files. No accounts, no cloud, just a quiet little tool for your reading life.

Download BookLoom 1.0 View on GitHub

Features

Multiple Shelves

Want-to-read, Reading, Read, and DNF (Did Not Finish). Move books between shelves as your reading changes.

Progress & DNF

Update your current page, mark books as finished with a date, or mark them as DNF directly from the progress view.

Tags & Categories

Add tags and a category (e.g., Fantasy, Horror, Non-fiction) to each book to keep your library organized.

Ratings & Favorites

Rate books from 1–5 stars and mark favorites. Use the random picker to grab a book from a shelf or by tag.

Series Tracking

Track series name and order, and mark entire series as complete when you’re done.

Themes

Choose from multiple color themes defined in themes.json, and switch themes in the Settings menu.

Different Themes

BookLoom includes multiple cozy color themes you can switch between in the Settings menu.

Requirements

  • Python 3.10 or later
  • Windows, macOS, or Linux terminal

On Windows, you can use the included run.bat file to open BookLoom with a comfortable window size.
